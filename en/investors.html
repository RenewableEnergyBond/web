Context & Opportunity

The digital asset market has grown rapidly but remains dominated by volatile instruments (cryptocurrencies) or low-yield products (stablecoins).

Investors are now seeking digital assets backed by real-world assets, combining security, yield, and ESG alignment.

rebond addresses this demand by digitalizing bonds issued by renewable energy producers.

Our Investment Offer

Digitalized Bonds

Financial instruments issued and managed on blockchain, backed by real projects and offering a contractual yield above 6%, with full transparency and traceability.

Tangible Underlying

Each bond directly finances solar or wind power plants, making a measurable contribution to the energy transition.

Secure Distribution

Investment reserved for qualified actors, with custody ensured by a regulated depositary bank and strict compliance procedures (KYC/AML).
